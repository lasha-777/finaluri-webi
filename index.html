A little about Georgia

Georgia is a country of unforgettable memories. Blessed with fascinating nature, sunny beaches, caves, abundant wildlife, canyons and culture. This small country has everything. It caters to everyone's interest, taste, budget and age. Even an all-seeing traveler will find it difficult to deny the country's beauty. Because Georgia is different from all other countries, it gives you a unique experience that is almost impossible to get anywhere else. Georgia will delight adventure seekers. There are many reasons why this small country of the Caucasus is worth visiting

A little WAT I KNOW

The area of the country is up to 70 thousand km2, and the population, as of January 1, 20, is almost 3.7 million. The population is multi-ethnic, with large ethnic minorities. For example, the ethnic Azerbaijani community is 6.2% of the population, and Armenians - 4.5%.



Georgia belongs to the group of developing economies, with a gross domestic product of $5015 per capita. Tourism plays an important role in the growth of the country's economy. The number of tourists, if we do not count the period of the pandemic, exceeds the number of the local population by more than two times for years.

Stretching from the coast of the Black Sea to the snowy peaks of the Caucasus, Georgia is visited by more and more tourists from many countries of the world every year. If in 2015 a total of 6,305,635 international visitors came to Georgia, which was a record at that time, in 2019 the number of international visitors broke the all-time record and amounted to 9,357,964 people. Then came the pandemic 2020, and the tourism industry, both in the whole world and in Georgia, practically stopped. Many people employed in the sector became unemployed and businesses went bankrupt. But in 2022, the industry began to revive and in the period of January-October 5,426,903 visitors came to Georgia. This means that the pre-pandemic 2019 record figure has recovered by 58%.
